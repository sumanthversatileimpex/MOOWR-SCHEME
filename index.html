About MOOWR

What is the
MOOWR Scheme?

The MOOWR Scheme (Manufacture and Other Operations in a Warehouse Regulations, 2019) is a flagship initiative by the Indian government that provides duty-deferred manufacturing and warehousing benefits to businesses. Designed to boost ease of doing business in India, the MOOWR scheme enables manufacturers to store and process imported goods without the burden of upfront customs duty and IGST payments. Businesses looking to obtain a MOOWR license across any part of India can leverage the scheme benefits as under.

Benefits

Key Benefits

Zero Upfront Duty

No upfront payment of custom duty and IGST on imported goods (capital goods & raw materials).

Seamless Import & Storage

Take immediate release of imported goods into a Manufacturing facility.

Improved Cash Flow

Duty payment is deferred until the goods are cleared for domestic sale, allowing businesses to optimize capital utilization. No duty interest payable.

No Export Obligations

Unlike other duty exemption schemes, MOOWR does not require to commit to export quantum.

No Bank Guarantee Required

Avail benefits without Advance License, EPCG, or AEO status nor EOU.

Flexible Manufacturing & Sales

Use imported goods for manufacturing, processing, or storage without any time restrictions.

Duty Payment Only on Domestic Sales

Pay customs duty/IGST only on the quantity of raw materials used for domestic sales.

No Interest on Deferred Duty

Businesses enjoy interest-free duty deferment, improving financial efficiency.

No Past Track Record Needed

New businesses can also benefit as past import/export history is not required. Over 1800 importers / manufacturers are already benefiting from the scheme.

Unlimited Storage Duration

No time limit for usage and clearance of imported goods, reducing inventory pressure.

Eligibility

Who can apply?

The MOOWR scheme is ideal for:

  • Manufacturers & Importers across India looking to import capital goods/raw materials without the financial burden of upfront duty.
  • Indian Businesses catering to both domestic and export markets, as the scheme has no restrictions on domestic sales.
  • Start-ups, Established Enterprises & Companies across diverse industries or sectors in India like engineering, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, automobiles, textiles, packaging, cement, and more.
